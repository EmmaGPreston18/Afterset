# Afterset 🎵

**The app that captures your concert memories.**

After the last note fades and the crowd quiets down—that's Afterset. The place where you log every show you attend, every moment you remember, every song that moved you.

## What is Afterset?

Afterset is your personal concert archive. Sign in, log the shows you attend, add photos and videos, rate your favorite performances, and keep all your live music memories in one place.

## Features

✨ **Log Your Shows**
- Artist/band name
- Venue location
- Date attended
- Star rating (1-5 stars)

📸 **Upload Photos & Videos**
- Add photos from the concert
- Record video moments
- View your memories in a beautiful gallery

🎵 **Track Setlists**
- Log the songs performed
- Keep a record of what you heard
- Remember the flow of the night

📝 **Add Notes**
- Write down memorable moments
- Capture the feeling of the show
- Share your experience

🔍 **View All Your Shows**
- Browse your concert history
- See how many shows you've attended
- Relive your favorite moments

## How to Use

1. **Create an Account** - Sign up with your email
2. **Log a Show** - Click "+ Log Show" button
3. **Fill in Details** - Artist, venue, date, and rating
4. **Add Media** - Upload photos or videos from the concert
5. **Save & Remember** - Your show is now in your Afterset archive!

## Getting Started

### Quick Start
1. Open `index.html` in your web browser
2. Create an account with your email
3. Start logging your concerts!

### Requirements
- Modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)
- Internet connection (for Supabase authentication)

## Technologies Used

- **Frontend:** HTML, CSS, JavaScript
- **Backend:** Supabase (Database & Authentication)
- **Storage:** Supabase Storage (for photos & videos)

## About Afterset

Afterset is built for concert lovers who want to remember every show. Whether you're a casual concertgoer or have attended hundreds of shows, Afterset helps you capture and celebrate the live music moments that matter to you.
